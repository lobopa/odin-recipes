Muffin recipe

What could be nicer than warm muffins in a napkin on the morning breakfast table?

And they are quick and easy to make , particularly since the ingredients are only lightly mixed, not beaten smooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup melted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Butter muffin pans.
  3. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  4. Add the egg, milk, and butter, stirring only enough to dampen the flour; the batter should not be smooth.
  5. Spoon into the muffin pans, filling each cup about two-thirds full. Bake for about 20 to 25 minutes each.
